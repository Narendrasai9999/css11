Chicken Wings Biryani

REQUIREMENTS

  • 800g chicken wings / nibbles
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Sea salt
  • 2 tbsp rice bran oil or similar
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 thumb ginger, julienne
  • 1 green chilli, sliced
  • ½ cup curry paste
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups Campbell’s Real Stock Chicken
  • ½ cup fresh pineapple, diced
