Sindhi Biryani

  • 1 Cup = 200 ml
  • Basmati rice - 1 cup (I use India Gate Brand)
  • Water - 6 cups
  • Whole garam masala - 1 each (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf, and black stone flower)
  • Salt - as needed
  • Mixed vegetables – 1 cup (saute in 1 tbsp butter and pressure cook)
  • To Grind:
  • Ripe tomato – 2 nos
  • Green chilli – 2 nos
  • For Sindhi masala:
  • Ghee or cooking oil – 3 tbsp
  • Cinnamon - 1 inch
  • Cloves - 2 nos
  • Cardamom - 1 no
  • Bay leaf - 1 no
  • Big onion - 1 no (sliced)
  • Ginger & Garlic Paste - 1 tbsp
  • Sindhi Biryani Masala Powder - 1.5 - 2 tbsp
  • Mint+coriander leaves - handful
  • Curd - 1/4 cup
  • Salt - as needed
  • To garnish:
  • Saffron thread - few
  • Milk - 1/4 cup
  • Mustard oil - 2 tbsp
